Introduction

He is a lecturer at Can Tho University as well as a research engineer, he is passionate and curious in software development, Big Data, distributed system, and cutting-edge technologies

In 2020, he obtained PhD degree in Computer Science at University of Rennes 1 under the supervision of Prof. Laurent d'Orazio and Assoc. Prof. Tristan Allard. His research interests include Database, IoT, Big Data and its applications, Data privacy and cyber-security, Modeling and Simulation.
